How It Works

Rafeeq is dedicated to helping animals find their forever homes.
Our platform allows individuals to post their pets for adoption
while also showcasing animals directly under Rafeeq’s care.
Each adoption involves a small fee, which helps cover care
and medical expenses.
Fees vary based on the type of animal and their age, ensuring
a fair process for all adopters.

Below is the detailed fee structure for adopting pets through Rafeeq:
