STAR (Special Treatment & Recovery)

Dogs and cats requiring extensive medical and/or surgical care often arrive at ACC. The STAR (Special Treatment & Recovery) Fund is used to pay for medical care that only a specialized facility can provide.

Community Pets Fund

The Community Pets Fund helps keep pets in loving homes by providing items such as free wellness services, spay/neuter surgeries, a pet food pantry and behavior training for owners in need.

Support ACC's Lifesaving Funds

We will happily accept your second-hand pet items. We ask that any used items are cleaned prior to donating. Rafeeq can accept:
  • Unopened and unexpired food and treats
  • Blankets, Towels, Sheets
  • Leashes and Harnesses
  • Crates and Carriers
  • Enrichment Toys
  • Unopened Litter, Pellets
Our care centers accept donations daily between 10AM and 5PM.
Please note we cannot accept open/expired food and treats, medications, pillows or mattresses.

Donate your gently used bath sized towels, sheets and linens so we can keep our animals warm and cozy while they await their forever homes!

Send needed items directly to ACC from our Wish List and you can help feed and comfort the Boroughbreds in our care.
